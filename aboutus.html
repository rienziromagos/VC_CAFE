Our Story

VC Cafe, founded in 2023, aims to bring Melbourne's vibrant cafe culture to Cebu through a unique mobile coffee and bar concept. With a focus on high-quality, ethically sourced coffee, handmade food, freshly baked bread, and artisanal pastries, VC Cafe strives to create meaningful experiences and connections. As athlete-owners, they understand the importance of both quality fuel and genuine community. Their mobile concept allows them to bring these experiences to various locations, creating pop-up communities throughout Cebu, and offering a welcoming space for coffee lovers and casual visitors alike.
